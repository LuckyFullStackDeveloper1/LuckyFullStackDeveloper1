NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) is a medical entrance exam for students who want to pursue MBBS, BDS, and other medical courses.

JEE Advanced is the second phase of the JEE exam for students aiming for IITs. It is one of the toughest engineering entrance exams in India.
